## Identified Integration Gaps (P1 Channels)

1. **fleet-simulator → plato-torch**: Simulation events could be formatted as `Tile` objects and submitted to a room (e.g., `SimulationRoom` preset). This would turn simulation runs into training data.

2. **holodeck-rust → plato-torch**: NPC-player interactions could be captured as tiles with sentiment metadata. Requires a bridge service (Rust→Python) or embedding tile submission in holodeck.

3. **plato-ensign ← fleet-simulator/holodeck**: Direct export from simulation/MUD to ensigns bypassing plato-torch. Would require tile serialization in Rust.

4. **GhostInjector integration**: The `GhostInjector` (from memory context) is not present in any inspected code. Could be a planned component to insert ghost tiles into rooms.
