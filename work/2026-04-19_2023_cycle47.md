## 2. Integration Gaps (What's Not Connected)

### **Gap 1: holodeck-rust → plato-ensign** (Missing Direct Path)
- **Problem:** NPC interactions from holodeck aren't documented as feeding into ensign training
- **Impact:** Rich sentiment-aware NPC data isn't directly creating specialist models
- **Priority:** Medium (would enrich training diversity)

### **Gap 2: fleet-simulator → plato-ensign** (Missing Bypass Path)
- **Problem:** Simulator data must go through plato-torch rooms first
- **Impact:** No direct ensign creation from simulated scenarios
- **Priority:** Low (current pipeline works, but less flexible)

### **Gap 3: constraint-theory-core → Any Training System** (Isolated Component)
- **Problem:** Geometric snapping library isn't connected to training pipelines
- **Impact:** Valuable constraint-solving patterns aren't captured as training tiles
- **Priority:** High (untapped P0/P1/P2 pattern source)

### **Gap 4: cudaclaw/flux-runtime → plato-torch** (Runtime Disconnect)
- **Problem:** GPU-resident agent runtime and bytecode VM aren't feeding training
- **Impact:** Runtime execution patterns aren't captured as learning
- **Priority:** Medium (performance optimization patterns lost)
