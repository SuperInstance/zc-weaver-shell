## 6. Integration Gaps & Recommendations  
**P0 (Negative Space):** Do not assume tile formats are compatible without validation.  
**P1 (Safe Channels):**  
1. **Holodeck-Rust REST API** — Could be called by Plato-Torch to ingest tiles.  
2. **Shared Tile Schema** — Define a common JSON schema for tiles across repos.  
3. **Plato-Ensign Adapter Injection** — Could inject LoRA adapters into Holodeck-Rust NPC models (requires Rust/Python bridge).  

**P2 (Optimization):**  
- Implement a `TileBridge` service that translates between Plato-Torch and Holodeck-Rust tile formats.  
- Add `import_from_holodeck` method to Plato-Torch tile loader.  
- Extend Plato-Ensign to export directly to Holodeck-Rust model format.
