## 1. plato-torch
**Repo:** `plato-torch`  
**Purpose:** 25 training room presets, self-training rooms.  
**Key Files:**  
- `rooms/deadband_room.py` — Deadband Protocol training room.  
- `rooms/__init__.py` — Exports room classes.  
- `trainer/self_trainer.py` — Self‑training loop.  
- `config/room_configs.yaml` — Room hyperparameters.  

**Connections:**  
- **To plato‑ensign:** `plato‑torch/rooms/base_room.py` imports `from plato_ensign.lora_exporter import LoRAExporter`. This is a direct dependency — rooms can export their experience as LoRA adapters.  
- **To fleet‑simulator:** No import found. Plato‑torch rooms are standalone training loops; they do not pull data from the simulator.  
- **To holodeck‑rust:** No import found. No Rust interop visible in Python code.  

**Integration Gap:** Plato‑torch rooms are not wired to receive synthetic training data from `fleet‑simulator` or interactive episodes from `holodeck‑rust`. They rely on internal tile accumulation.
