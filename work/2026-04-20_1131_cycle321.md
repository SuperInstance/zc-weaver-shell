## 1. plato-torch
**Location:** `plato-torch/`  
**Purpose:** 25 training room presets, self-training rooms.  
**Key Files:**  
- `rooms/deadband_room.py` — Deadband Protocol training room.  
- `rooms/__init__.py` — Room registry.  
- `tile_server.py` — Receives and validates training tiles.  
- `ensign_exporter.py` — Exports room experience to LoRA adapters.  

**Connections:**  
- **plato-ensign:** Direct import in `ensign_exporter.py`. Uses `PlatoEnsign` class to export LoRA adapters.  
- **fleet-simulator:** No direct import found. Potential integration via tile data format.  
- **holodeck-rust:** No direct import.  
- **Shared Data:** Training tiles (JSON) are the primary data flow.  

## 2. plato-ensign

## 5. Integration Gaps (What's Not Connected)  
- **plato-torch → fleet-simulator:** No direct pipeline for converting simulation logs to training tiles.  
- **plato-torch → holodeck-rust:** No ingestion of NPC interactions into training rooms.  
- **fleet-simulator → plato-ensign:** Simulation data not used for LoRA training.  
- **holodeck-rust → plato-ensign:** Sentiment-aware NPC experiences not exported as adapters.  

## 6. Potential Integration Points  
1. **Tile Bridge:** Format fleet-simulator logs and holodeck-rust interactions as training tiles for plato-torch ingestion.  
2. **GhostInjector:** Could feed ghost tiles (P0 knowledge) into holodeck-rust NPC memory.  
3. **Ensemble Training:** Use simulation data as a training source for plato-ensign LoRA adapters.  
4. **Sentiment-Aware Rooms:** Extend DeadbandRoom with sentiment logic from holodeck-rust.
