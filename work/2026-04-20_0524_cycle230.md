## 1. plato-torch (Training Rooms)
**Location:** `plato-torch/`  
**Purpose:** 25 training room presets, self-training rooms.  
**Key Files:**

- `rooms/__init__.py` — Room registry.  
- `config/room_configs.yaml` — Room hyperparameters.  

**Connections:**  
- **To plato-ensign:** Exports room experience via `export_lora_adapter()` in `deadband_room.py`.

- **To holodeck-rust:** No direct import found.  

**Integration Points:**  
- `deadband_room.py` calls `plato_ensign.export.export_lora_adapter()` (line 147).

## 2. plato-ensign (LoRA Export)  
**Location:** `plato-ensign/`  
**Purpose:** Exports room experience into LoRA adapters.  
**Key Files:**  
- `export/lora_exporter.py` — LoRA export logic.  
- `config/export_config.yaml` — Export settings.  

**Connections:**  
- **From plato-torch:** Imported in `deadband_room.py`.  
- **To fleet-simulator:** No direct import.  
- **To holodeck-rust:** No direct import.  

**Integration Points:**  
- `lora_exporter.py` expects a `room_state` dict with `tiles` and `hyperparameters`.
