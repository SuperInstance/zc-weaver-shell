1. **holodeck-rust ↔ plato-torch**
   - **Gap:** No live data flow. NPC interactions not captured as training tiles.
   - **Risk:** Sentiment-aware NPC experiences lost for ensign training.
   - **P0 Action:** Do NOT assume connection exists.

2. **fleet-simulator → holodeck-rust**
   - **Gap:** Simulated agents cannot interact with MUD world.
   - **Risk:** Training data lacks real-time multi-agent interaction.
   - **P0 Action:** Do NOT wire without protocol bridge.

3. **plato-ensign ← holodeck-rust**
   - **Gap:** NPC sentiment patterns not exported to LoRA adapters.
   - **Risk:** Specialized "NPC behavior" ensigns cannot be trained.
   - **P0 Action:** Do NOT modify export pipeline without tile format extension.
