## 1. plato-torch → fleet-simulator
**Connection:** **Strong** (direct import, shared data structures)
- **plato-torch/src/rooms/deadband_room.py** imports `from fleet_simulator.simulator import FleetSimulator`
- **plato-torch/src/rooms/deadband_room.py** line 47: `self.simulator = FleetSimulator()` — instantiated as part of room initialization.
- **Shared data:** `Tile` objects (defined in plato-torch) are passed to the simulator's `record_tile()` method.
- **Integration point:** `DeadbandRoom` uses the simulator to log training interactions (tiles) for later replay/analysis.

**Gap:** The simulator appears to be a passive recorder in this flow. No evidence of the simulator actively influencing room behavior or training logic.

## 2. plato-torch → plato-ensign
**Connection:** **Strong** (direct import, core dependency)
- **plato-torch/src/rooms/base_room.py** imports `from plato_ensign.export import export_ensign`
- **plato-torch/src/rooms/base_room.py** line 89-102: `export_room()` method calls `export_ensign()` with room state and accumulated tiles.
- **Shared data:** Entire room object (`self`) and tile history are passed to ensign exporter.
- **Integration point:** Room training culminates in optional ensign (LoRA) export.

**Gap:** Export is optional (`if self.config.get('export_ensign'):`). Not all rooms may produce ensigns.
