## 2. Integration Gaps (What's Not Connected)

### **Gap 1: holodeck-rust → plato-ensign** (Missing Export Path)
- **Problem:** NPC interactions in holodeck don't automatically become ensigns
- **Impact:** Sentiment-aware NPC experience trapped in MUD, not exported
- **Priority:** **High** — Wastes valuable training data

### **Gap 2: GhostInjector → holodeck-rust** (P0 Knowledge Not Injected)
- **Problem:** Ghost tiles (dead agents' lessons) not feeding into holodeck NPCs
- **Impact:** NPCs can't learn from fleet's negative space mapping
- **Priority:** **Medium** — Reduces NPC intelligence growth

### **Gap 3: DeadbandRoom → plato-relay** (No Real-time Feedback)
- **Problem:** Deadband Protocol training isolated from relay system
- **Impact:** Can't test P0/P1/P2 decisions in live fleet context
- **Priority:** **High** — Limits protocol validation

### **Gap 4: constraint-theory-core → fleet-simulator** (Geometric Rules Not Applied)
- **Problem:** Rust geometric snapping library not integrated with simulator
- **Impact:** Simulation lacks constraint-based movement/positioning
- **Priority:** **Medium** — Reduces simulation realism
