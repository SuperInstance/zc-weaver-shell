## 6. Identified Gaps (P0 / P1)

**P0 (Negative space — what NOT to do):**
- Do not assume holodeck‑rust feeds plato‑torch automatically.
- Do not assume fleet‑simulator logs are ingested without a tile‑formatter.
- Do not call `export_lora` without a trained room model.

**P1 (Safe channels — where you CAN be):**
- plato‑torch → plato‑ensign is a working, tested channel.
- fleet‑simulator logs can be manually formatted into tiles for plato‑torch.
- holodeck‑rust NPC logs could be formatted as sentiment‑training tiles.

**P2 (Optimization — best path):**
- Build a `LogToTile` adapter for fleet‑simulator `.jsonl` → plato‑torch tiles.
- Add a sentiment‑tile ingester in plato‑torch that reads holodeck‑rust logs.
- Create an integration test that runs: fleet‑simulator → tile → room → LoRA.
